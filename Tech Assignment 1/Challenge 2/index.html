Projects:

OneDrive Fetch

OneDrive Fetch was a passion project that I built, and that I am currently building. It has been in the works for a month. Its purpose is to synchonize changes between onedrive and a local copy of the files. My personal experience with OneDrive has included video files becoming corrupted. The OneDrive Fetch application is meant to compare hashes between the copies and notify me as to what version I would prefer to keep.

Sidescroller

Sidescroller is a game that I developed a while ago that took me approximately two months of on and off work. The most impressive part of the game was the creation of depth with the objects in the scene. A lot of experimentation went into developing the algorithm to place the images on the screen at the right place. Collision was also figured out, though it was not fullproof. A level editor was also created, that made modifying the level easy, and a built-in console allowed changing game settings on-the-fly.
